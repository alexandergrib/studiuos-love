Top 10 Romantic Movies

Everyone loves a romantic movie, right?

Here's what the Guardian and Observer's critics think are the 10 most romantic movies of all time.

  • 10. Jules et Jim

    Jules et Jim was the biggest box-office success the French New Wave ever enjoyed. When it opened in Paris in January 1962, it played for nearly three months and it found the same crowds all over the world.

  • 9. A Room With a View

    This is incredibly fresh and arresting film-making: moving and amusing, swooningly romantic and socially ferocious – nothing less than a full-frontal (in every way) assault on your soul.

  • 8. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

    The film's concept is that a couple can delete each other after a painful break-up so they can live on in blissful ignorance.

  • 7. Hannah and Her Sisters

    This 1986 roundelay grossed $40m and became Woody Allen's biggest ever box-office hit.

  • 6. The Apartment

    It won five Academy Awards, including best picture, best director and best screenplay.

  • 5. In the Mood for Love

    It is often listed as one of the greatest films of all time and one of the major works of Asian cinema.

  • 4. Breathless (A Bout de Souffle)

    Upon its initial release in France, the film attracted over two million viewers. It has since become considered one of the best films ever made.

  • 3. Before Sunrise/Before Sunset/Before Midnight

    The Before trilogy received widespread critical acclaim, nominated for two Academy Awards, two Writers Guild of America Awards, and the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Motion Picture Comedy or Musical for Delpy for Before Midnight. The series is also a commercial success, grossing $48.8 million worldwide against a combined budget of $8.8 million.

  • 2. Casablanca

    Casablanca stands for movie romance in great part because it is hardly true to life.

  • 1. Brief Encounter

    Brief Encounter is etched in nostalgia for an era when trapped middle-class lives contemplated adultery but set the disturbing thought aside.

Top 10 Romantic Places in Ireland

Rain or shine, the rolling green hills of the Emerald Isle are an idyllic, romantic place. After all, there’s nothing better than a lovely Irish sunset or a bit of dancing in the rain!

  • 10 Summer sunset bike ride along Sky Road

    If you’re in Connemara with your sweetheart, a perfect way to spend a summer evening is to ride bikes along the aptly-named Sky Road.

  • 9 Greet St Valentine in Dublin’s Whitefriar Street Church

    Whitefriar Street Church, founded in the 13th century, is the final resting place of St Valentine!

  • 8 A beachside picnic at quiet beaches like Sliver Stand

    For a truly romantic experience, picnic at a Mayo’s Silver Stand beach, where you can listen to the waves, taste the salt in the air, and have the sand to yourself!

  • 7 Hiking Ben Bulben’s Gleniff Horseshoe

    This magical valley is alive with stories and legends, but the one that springs to mind is the tragic love story of Diarmuid and Grainne, mythological lovers eternally pursed by an angry giant, who met their ends under Ben Bulben’s shadow in the real-world Cave of Diarmuid and Gráinne.

  • 6 Learn about the Claddagh Ring in Galway

    The Claddagh Ring, associated with Galway, it is a traditional Irish ring made from two clasped hands holding a crown. The ring is said to represent love, loyalty, and friendship.

  • 5 Horseback at dawn on Streedagh Beach

    Enjoy a beachside horseback ride, perfect for admiring the sunset on the horizon.

  • 4 Moonlight kayak in Cork’s bio-luminescent water

    For a truly unique and magical experience with your partner, do a moonlight kayak in Cork. From a dazzling sunset to a starry sky, this is lovely experience to share with your lover.

  • 3 Enjoy adorable Adare & elegant Adare Manor

    Stroll arm in arm with your other half through the adorable village of Adare, where cute thatched cottages house homey restaurants and snug shops.

  • 2 Jaunting cart/jarvey through the Gap of Dunloe

    A horse and carriage ride in a charming 19th century buggy through the wild beauty of the Gap of Dunloe in the heart of Killarney National Park – what could be more romantic than that?

  • 1 Cliffs of Moher at Sunset

    The Cliffs of Moher, an enduring classic. Visiting these massive and beloved cliffs is always a wondrous experience, but visiting with your true love is even better!
